Campaign Tracking Endpoints

ConstantContact.get_campaign_opens(campaign_id, created_since = None, limit = 500)
Returns a
Result_Set
of
Activity_Report
objects. campaign_id accepts a
Campaign
object or a string. created_since accepts an iso-8601 formatted time string for specifying after what date to retrieve activity reports. limit accepts an integer specifying the maximum number of activity reports per page.
